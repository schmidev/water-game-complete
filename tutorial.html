Tutorial

Drag the following items in this order to successfully create a DIY water filter:

  1. Knife (cut and flip the bottle)
  2. Cotton balls
  3. Charcoal
  4. Sand
  5. Gravel

Second Game Mode: Water Filter Collection

  • Click bottles with the correct filter layers (from bottom to top: Gravel, Sand, Charcoal, Cotton) to score points.
  • Each correct bottle gives +1000 points. Incorrect bottles deduct 500 points (minimum score is 0).
  • Only bottles with the correct order count as correct. Bottles appear and disappear in a 3x3 grid.
  • You have 30 seconds to score as many points as possible.
  • Try to beat your high score!
